Our major areas of specialization include oncology, orthopedics, cardiology, IVF Treatment, urology, neurosurgery, gastroenterology, plastic surgery and many other departments. Premium private hospital, provides the highest quality medical care, individualized treatment. As part of the hospitals uniquely personalized service, each patient is assigned a case manager to handle all administrative, logistical and medical issues.
